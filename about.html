Education

Trebas Institute
Toronto, Ontario

I attended Trebas Institute in Toronto, Ontario in 2003, enrolling in their Recorded Music Production program. The curriculum offered a comprehensive mix of audio engineering, music production, and music business. During my time at Trebas, I gained hands-on experience working with professional audio equipment in studio labs, most notably at the CBC studios in Toronto, where I trained in a real-world recording environment. I developed expert knowledge in audio engineering and post-production audio.

Fanshawe College
London, Ontario

Building on my strong background in media, the next natural step was enrolling in Fanshawe College’s Interactive Media Design program. The program is designed to develop multi-disciplinary contributors in the fields of media, web development, and design.
During my studies, I explored the fundamentals of 3D design, graphic design, and motion design, using industry-standard tools such as Adobe Creative Suite and Cinema 4D. I also gained a solid foundation in responsive web development, learning how these creative tools integrate with core programming languages used in modern web design.

Awards

Young Audio Visual Composer Award

January 2012 • Issued by SOCAN

In 2012, I received an industry award for composing the official theme song for the Canadian film Unrivaled. A fusion of rap and heavy rock, the track was crafted in close collaboration with the film’s director to match the movie’s intensity and tone. The award came through a nationwide competition and remains a proud milestone in my creative career.

President's Honour Roll

April, 2021 • Issued by Fanshawe College

I was named to the President’s Honour Roll at Fanshawe College for graduating with a cumulative GPA of 4.05. This distinction recognizes students who achieve academic excellence across the entire program, reflecting consistent performance, dedication, and mastery of their field. It was an honour to complete the program at the top of my class.
